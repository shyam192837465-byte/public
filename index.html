Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to the most common questions about clinic treatments and appointments.

What are the clinic timings of Dr. Saranya?
Dr. Saranya is available from Monday to Saturday during 09:30 AM to 01:30 PM (Morning session) and 04:30 PM to 08:30 PM (Evening session). The clinic remains closed on Sundays.
How do I schedule an appointment online?
You can easily book a slot using the booking form right above on this page. Just fill in your details and select a treatment. Our staff will call/SMS you back within a few hours to confirm the slot.
Is root canal treatment painful?
Not at all. With local anesthesia and modern rotary endodontic systems used by Dr. Saranya at SDC, root canal treatments are virtually pain-free. It feels similar to getting a standard dental filling.
Do you offer clear orthodontic aligners?
Yes, we offer clear teeth aligners, which are invisible, comfortable, and removable alternatives to conventional metallic braces. We will design a custom digital teeth alignment simulation for you.
